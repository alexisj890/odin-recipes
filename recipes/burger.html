Burger

Description

A juicy, flavorful homemade burger made with seasoned ground beef, grilled to perfection, and served on a toasted bun with fresh toppings. This classic burger features a tender patty, melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and tangy pickles, all topped with your favorite condiments. Perfect for a backyard barbecue or a quick meal, this burger is easy to make and packed with deliciousness

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles, onion
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps

  1. Make the Patties- Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Shape into 4 patties.
  2. Cook the Patties - Grill or pan-fry over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes per side, flipping once. Add cheese in the last minute.
  3. Toast the Buns - Lightly toast burger buns on the grill or pan until golden
  4. Assemble the Burger - Place the patty on the bun, add lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onion.
  5. Add Condiments & Serve - Top with ketchup, mustard, or mayo. Enjoy your homemade burger!
