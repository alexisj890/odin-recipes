Salad

Description

A fresh and crunchy Caesar salad made with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a creamy, tangy dressing. Tossed with Parmesan cheese and a homemade or store-bought Caesar dressing, this salad is simple yet packed with flavor. Perfect as a light meal or a side dish, it's a timeless favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Lettuce - Chop romaine lettuce and place it in a large bowl.
  2. Add Croutons & Cheese - Toss in croutons and sprinkle Parmesan cheese.
  3. Mix in Dressing - Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  4. Season & Serve - Add black pepper and a squeeze of lemon juice (optional). Enjoy!
