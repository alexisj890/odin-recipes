Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. Baked until golden and bubbly, it’s a hearty, comforting meal perfect for any occasion. Serve with garlic bread and a salad for a complete feast!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or sausage
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tsp basil & oregano
  • 1 egg
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles-Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce-In a pan, cook ground beef with chopped onion until browned. Add garlic, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Mix the Cheese Filling-In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna-Layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and paramesan cheese in a baking dish
  5. Bake-Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10-15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it Rest-Let lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
